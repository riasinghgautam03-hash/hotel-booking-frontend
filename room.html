A Suite Room is a luxurious and spacious accommodation designed to offer a higher level of comfort, privacy, and amenities compared to standard rooms. It typically includes multiple rooms within the same unit — such as a separate bedroom and living area, and sometimes a kitchenette or dining space.

A Double Bed Room is one of the most common room types in hotels. It features one double-sized bed designed to comfortably accommodate two people—perfect for couples, solo travelers who prefer extra space, or even business guests.

The Deluxe Room is elegantly designed for both comfort and style, featuring a spacious layout with modern décor. Guests can enjoy a plush king or queen-size bed, a well-appointed bathroom with premium toiletries, and conveniences such as high-speed Wi-Fi, a flat-screen TV, minibar, and a dedicated work desk. Large windows offer ample natural light, often with city or garden views. Ideal for business and leisure travelers alike, the Deluxe Room ensures a relaxing and refined stay.
